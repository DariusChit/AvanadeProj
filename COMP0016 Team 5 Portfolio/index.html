Abstract

With the rapid development of Artificial Intelligence, the trend leaded by OpenAI brings the public to be more willing to work with AI. However, it is unusual to have an AI assistant that is specifically designed for the legal industry. This is where JurisBUD AI comes in. By using the latest concept of AI agents, JuridsBUD AI can help legal professionals to solve problems that they might face in their carrer. Instead of working alone till midnight, searching online and reading 1000 pages of documents, you can now just aske JurisBUD AI and a whole team of AI agents will be at your service.

Features

Here are some of amazing features for our JurisBUD AI, not only the listed ones, it can always do more than you thought

01

Legal Querying

Have any questions in legal area? Just Ask! JurisBUD AI can solve any human language question by using our great large language model. There will always be a whole team of AI agents at your service.

02

Contract Drafting

JurisBUD AI can help legal professionals, especially lawyers, to draft a legal contract to start your writing. It can also provide you some ideas and references to help you finish your work.

02

Tasks Generating

JurisBUD AI uses the amazing concept of AI agents, which allow you to split your query into multiple tasks. If you are unhappy with current strategy, you can always ask for a new one in just one click.
